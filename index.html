Surf Brand Social Strategy

Developing a social media strategy to increase engagement and reach younger surf audiences through short-form video and athlete storytelling.

View Case Study ↗
Overview
This project focuses on developing a social media strategy to increase engagement and reach younger surf audiences through short-form video content and athlete storytelling.
Strategy
The strategy focuses on using Instagram Reels and lifestyle content to highlight surf culture, athlete personalities, and user-generated content.
  • Posting 4 short-form videos per week
  • Featuring surfers and lifestyle moments
  • Encouraging followers to post their own surf clips
Content Ideas
  • "Surfer of the Week" spotlight
  • Behind-the-scenes of surf sessions
  • Quick interview surf stories and tips from athletes
  • Repost relevant content from followers
Expected Results
This strategy can potentially increase engagement and audience interaction by creating relatable, authentic surf culture content that resonates with younger audiences.

04
Social Media Strategy

Kaua'i Salsa — Local Brand Strategy

A social media strategy to help a locally owned salsa business build an online presence, increase brand awareness, and attract both locals and tourists.

View Case Study ↗
Overview
This project focuses on developing a social media strategy to help a locally owned salsa business build an online presence, increase brand awareness, and attract both locals and tourists through engaging short-form video content.
Strategy
Using Instagram Reels and TikTok to showcase the product in a fun, lifestyle-driven way that fits the Kaua'i vibe — combining food, beach, and authentic local experiences.
  • Posting 3–5 short-form videos per week
  • Highlighting the freshness and uniqueness of the salsa
  • Using real customer reactions and community involvement
  • Leveraging trending audio and quick hooks to boost reach
Content Ideas
  • "Local Taste Test" reactions from around the island
  • Behind-the-scenes salsa making process
  • "Beach snack" and "After Surf snack" lifestyle clips
Expected Results
The strategy could increase engagement and brand visibility by creating authentic, visually appealing content that connects with both the local community and visitors to Kaua'i.
